About Eric

A Passion for Music,
A Gift for Teaching

Eric Chung is a classically trained pianist and passionate educator with over 16 years of teaching experience. A graduate of the Royal Conservatoire of Antwerp (Master of Music) 🇧🇪 and the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts in Classical Piano Performance, Eric brings deep musical knowledge and international training to every lesson.

Beyond teaching, Eric is an experienced accompanist for a wide range of instruments and choirs. At Cadenza London Music Academy, he offers a deeply personal and tailored approach — nurturing technique, musicality, and a lifelong love of music. Lessons can be conducted in English or Cantonese. 🗣️🇭🇰🇬🇧

How to Submit

Upload Your Practice Video

No special equipment needed — your phone camera is perfect. Choose whichever platform you're most comfortable with below, then paste the link into the feedback form.

▶️

YouTube

Recommended

  1. Open the YouTube app and tap ＋ Create
  2. Select your video and tap Upload
  3. Set visibility to "Unlisted" — only Eric can see it
  4. Copy the link and paste into the form below

💡 Why Unlisted? Your video won't appear in search or on your channel — it's completely private unless you share the link.

📁

Google Drive

Easiest for iPhone/Android

  1. Open Google Drive and tap ＋ New → Upload
  2. Select your video from your camera roll
  3. Tap the three dots → Share → Copy Link
  4. Set to "Anyone with the link" and paste below

💡 You get 15GB free with any Google account — plenty for practice videos.

📤

WeTransfer

No account needed

  1. Go to wetransfer.com on your phone or laptop
  2. Tap ＋ Add your files and select your video
  3. Choose "Get a link" (not email transfer)
  4. Copy the link and paste into the form below

⚠️ WeTransfer links expire after 7 days — submit your form promptly after uploading.

🎬

Tips for a Great Recording

✦Film in a quiet room with good natural light

✦Show both your hands and keyboard where possible

✦Film in landscape (horizontal) for the best view

✦Play the piece straight through — don't stop and restart

✦Sit in your normal playing posture — Eric will check it

✦You can submit multiple clips if you want feedback on different sections
