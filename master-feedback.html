Questions

Frequently Asked

Who gives the feedback?

All feedback is provided by Eric, a conservatoire-trained musician and experienced teacher with years of performance and teaching experience at all levels.

What format should I submit my video in?

Any standard video format works — MP4, MOV, AVI. You can film on a smartphone; professional recording equipment is not required. Good lighting and a steady shot help, but we care about your playing, not production quality.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. We welcome students of all levels — from early beginner through to diploma and beyond. Eric tailors feedback to your current stage and goals.

Can I submit a piece I'm preparing for an exam?

Yes — exam preparation is one of the most common use cases. Eric understands ABRSM and Trinity syllabi and can give targeted feedback aligned with exam criteria.

How is this different from a regular lesson?

Video feedback is asynchronous — you submit when you're ready, and receive considered, focused feedback without the time pressure of a live lesson. It's ideal as a complement to lessons, a second opinion, or for students without easy access to a teacher.
